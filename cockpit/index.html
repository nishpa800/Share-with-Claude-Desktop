The Four Structures — everything diagnoses these

Every indicator, every detection plot, every agent has ONE job: classify where on the spectrum these four structures sit. Output is always BUY or SHORT.

⚡ Potential Energy

Max stored energy on a candle — the extreme bearish (for longs) structure about to convert. The rocket fuel.

🚀 Kinetic Energy

The propulsion moment. The release. Different types — we care about full-jet-fuel kinetic right next to its potential source.

🏗️ Support Platform

The floor. VOB / order block / propulsion block. How anti-fragile is it? Will it break?

🧱 Resistance

The ceiling. The opposing structure. The asymmetry between floor and ceiling is the opportunity.
